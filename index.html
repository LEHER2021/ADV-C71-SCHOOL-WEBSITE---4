DELHI PUBLIC SCHOOL Secunderabad

“Colour is the place where our brain and universe meet” - PaulKlee The team of Class I had organised an event filled with colours for mothers of Grade I. The school premises wore a colourful ambience with this event ‘Fresco De Colours’ on Saturday, 16 November 2019 at the school premises. The event started at 9.00A.M by a warm welcome from the students of Grade Ito all the participants. The event commenced with the address of our Principal Ma’am, Ms. Shailaja Gopinath. They were divided into groups having different colour bows. The colours were red, blue, yellow, greenand orange.Each participant was given these colour bows near the reception to identify their team. The groups were arranged into Seven games for the participants. The games were as follows: 1. Caterpillar race 2. Stepping stones 3. Mask making 4. Hula hoops 5. Copy me 6. Treasure hunt 7. Tug of War All the participants participated in theabove-mentioned games had different number of participants. The enthusiastic mothers participated in all the games with great vigour. The students of Grade I were excited to see their mother participate in the fun-filled colourful games and applauded as their mothers showcased their talents. This special event was a part of the other activities observed during the Fit India Celebration Week. Lastlydue to the enthusiasm of the parents, we came up with an on-the-spotTUG OF WAR game organised by the Physical Education Department team. Theevent was a fun filled event. Mothers had a great time and they were all very happy as they re-lived their childhood days. We the team of grade I were really happy to see a big smile on the mothers’ face. Our pupils were more excited to see their mothers play. The event concluded with the prize distribution to all the winning teams and participants. The teachers of Grade Prep, Grade IIas well as our P.E. teachers lend their support and help to make this fun-filled event a great success. We thankourPrincipal Ma’am, Ms. Shailaja Gopinath for making this event possible for the mothers of Grade I. Our heartfelt thanks to all the mothers for their active participation. Kudos to all the mothers of Grade I for their involvement!! ****** - Ms. Bijal Mehta Grade IThe Delhi Public School Society took shape in 1949, with a long march to independence to fulfil a social, national need for young educated citizens to take India into the future. From the beginning in the President's Estate in 1941 as Church High School to Naveen Bharat School in 1947 and finally in 1949 at its present location on Mathura Road, DPS has traversed a long journey. Guided by the cause of pioneering quality education for India’s deserving young minds, the society embarked on establishing many a premier school across the country From the first school established in 1949 the DPS family has today grown to a movement of more than 200 schools even in the remotest, most inaccessible parts of the country and abroad. Innovation is fundamental to DPS learning programmes and its faculty has endeavoured to revolutionize in terms of ideas and practices, to match the demands of twenty- first century educational trends and global associations. The primary focus is on meaningful and joyful learning. Academia from the country and abroad, meet to exchange and partake of diverse cultural and intellectual experiences. Educational programmes evolve from the felt needs, incorporating contextual issues and challenges. Activities and events related to value education, environment, health education, games and sports, visual and performing arts, culture and heritage, international experience sharing, personality development etc. are effectively woven into the main fabric of school curriculum. Launched on 07 December 2002, Delhi Public School, Secunderabad was inaugurated on July 08 2003. The school commenced with a mere 192 students who were led to school by eager and enthusiastic parents. The then Chairman of DPS Society Shri. Narendra Kumar laid the foundation stone in the presence of Prof. Umma Reddy Venkateshwarlu garu, Chairman, Vidyananda Educational Society. The DPS Society is headed by Shri. Ashok Chandra under whose stewardship the school has grown from strength to strength. Luminaries like Mr. Pramod Grover, Prof. B.P. Khandelwal and Admiral Chopra provide direction and support from time to time..
